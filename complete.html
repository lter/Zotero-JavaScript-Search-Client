For more than 40 years, the scientists, students, and staff of the Long Term Ecological Research Network have observed, experimented, analyzed and modeled the dynamics of ecosystems representing most major US biomes. They have produced over 24,000 papers, theses, and books.

This search interface accesses the LTER Network Zotero group library (ID 2055673), which is updated monthly with new journal articles and annually with theses and books that have been directly supported by National Science Foundation funding through the LTER Program.

Many additional publications were not directly supported, but were only possible because of the data, experiments, and collaborations of the LTER Network. Those are not reported here, but can often be found in bibliographies associated with individual sites.
